FDM 3D Printing

FDM 3d printing is the process of constructing a three dimensional object from a 3d model. This is being done by slowly depositing tiny layers of plastic from a filament spool, such process allows the person owning the printer to design a part and print it within a few hours, allowing to produce prototypes of many mechanical/art/robotics designs at very attractive costs, combining with the lighting speed prototyping capabilities a printer unlocks, it becomes a very usefull tool for pretty much everyone, wether youre a large company or a hobbyist however- as for everything, a FDM printer has its own set of disadvantages, we will be discussing some of them.
