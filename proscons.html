Advantages and disadvantages

These are some of the advantages and disadvantages of a desktop FDM printer.

Advantages

Cost

A basic desktop 3d printer can cost around 200$ up to 1000$

Speed

Due to the ease of designing a 3D object, an idea can turn into reality within few hours.

Material choice

There are many kinds of filaments supported by many printers, such filaments can be PLA, Nylon, carbon fiber, ABS, PETG, and many more!

Disadvantages

Strength

The strength of a 3d printed part will always be less than if it was produced by a production line due to the layers created in the process.

Setting up

Setting up your printer for the first time can be quite a hustle, as many things needs to be done such as bed balancing.

maintenance

A 3d printer should always be kept clean and free of any debris, sometimes it can be quite tedious to clean it.
